Our Care Services

We offer a comprehensive range of care options tailored to meet the unique needs of each resident, ensuring comfort, safety, and peace of mind for families.

Independent Living

Enjoy maintenance-free living with the freedom to pursue your passions. Our independent living apartments offer resort-style amenities, social activities, and the security of knowing help is always nearby when needed.

Assisted Living

Receive personalized support with daily activities while maintaining your independence. Our caring staff provides assistance with medication management, bathing, dressing, and other needs in a warm, home-like environment.

Memory Care

Specialized care for residents with Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia. Our secure, purpose-built community features evidence-based programs, trained caregivers, and therapeutic activities designed to enhance quality of life.

Skilled Nursing

Round-the-clock medical care from licensed nurses and healthcare professionals. We provide comprehensive rehabilitation services, chronic disease management, and post-surgical recovery support in a comfortable setting.

Respite Care

Short-term stays for seniors who need temporary care while family caregivers take a break. Enjoy all our community amenities and services while your loved ones rest and recharge, knowing you're in excellent hands.

Transportation

Complimentary transportation services for medical appointments, shopping trips, and community outings. Our accessible vehicles and friendly drivers ensure you can stay connected to the world outside our community.

About Our Community

A Place to Call Home

For over 25 years, Sunrise Senior Living has been dedicated to providing exceptional care and enriching experiences for seniors in our community. Our mission is simple: to champion quality of life for all seniors.

Nestled on 15 beautifully landscaped acres, our community offers the perfect blend of comfort, care, and connection. From our chef-prepared meals to our engaging activities program, every detail is designed with your well-being in mind.

Our team of compassionate caregivers, nurses, and activity coordinators work together to create a warm, supportive environment where residents can thrive. We believe that aging should be a time of growth, discovery, and joy.
